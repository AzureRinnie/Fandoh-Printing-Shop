The Fandoh Story

Fandoh's Printing Shop was born out of a simple mission: to provide the Cebu community with reliable, high-quality document services. What started as helping friends with school projects has grown into a dedicated hub for students and professionals alike.

We’ve spent countless hours perfecting our process—testing paper weights, ink densities, and machine settings—to ensure every print is sharp and vibrant. Our signature Black and Orange colors reflect the energy, precision, and hard work we put into every page.

Whether you're rushing a thesis deadline or printing marketing materials for a new business, we treat your work with the care it deserves. At Fandoh's, we don't just print; we help you get your best work done. Sulit, sigurado, at de-kalidad.
