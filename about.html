The Fandoh Story

My name is Prince Mark, and I started Fandoh's Printing Shop because I saw how much people in our community needed a reliable place for their documents. It started with me helping out friends with their school projects and realizing that a good print job can make a huge difference.

I’ve spent a lot of time testing different papers and settings to make sure every page comes out clean and vibrant. Whether it’s a single black-and-white form or a full-color poster, I treat every order with the same care. I chose Black and Orange as my colors because they represent the energy and hard work I put into this shop every day

At the end of the day, Fandoh's is about helping you get your work done. If you have a deadline or a project you're proud of, I’m here to help you get it printed exactly how you want it. Sulit and high-quality.
